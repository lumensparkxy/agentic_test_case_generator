# Sample Requirements

## Authentication
- The system shall allow users to sign in using email and password.
- The system shall lock the account after 5 failed login attempts within 10 minutes.
- The system shall allow users to reset their password via email link.

## Profile
- The system shall allow users to update their display name.
- The system shall allow users to upload a profile photo (JPG or PNG, max 2MB).

## Search
- The system shall allow users to search by keyword.
- Search results shall be sorted by relevance by default.
- The system shall show a "no results" message when no matches are found.

## Notifications
- The system shall allow users to opt in to email notifications.
- The system shall send a confirmation email when a user changes their email address.
